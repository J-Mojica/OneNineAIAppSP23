AirBnB Prices

AirBnB prices can fluctuate based on a number of different factors such as location, time of year, and number of guests, number of rooms, etc. An AI model that is able to predict the price of an AirBnB listing based on such characteristic can be helpful for hosts to check that the price of their listing is competitive and for guests to find the best price for their stay.
